About Us

Our story, mission, and work supporting Ukraine

Our Mission

Witney Ukraine Support was established in [MONTH YEAR] following the Russian invasion of Ukraine. We are a community-led initiative in Witney, Oxfordshire, dedicated to providing practical support to Ukraine and Ukrainian refugees.

Our mission is to:

  • Provide immediate humanitarian aid to Ukraine
  • Support Ukrainian refugees settling in our community
  • Facilitate cultural exchange and integration
  • Raise awareness about the ongoing situation in Ukraine
  • Coordinate community efforts to maximize our impact

We work closely with local organizations, churches, businesses, and volunteers to create a welcoming and supportive environment for Ukrainians arriving in Witney.

Our Team

Witney Ukraine Support is run entirely by volunteers who give their time, expertise, and resources to help those affected by the war in Ukraine.

Our team includes:

  • Coordinators who manage donations and relief efforts
  • Volunteers who sort, pack, and transport aid
  • Host families who open their homes to Ukrainian refugees
  • Translators who help with communication
  • Local businesses providing resources and support

We're always looking for more volunteers to join our team. If you'd like to help, please contact us or take action today.
